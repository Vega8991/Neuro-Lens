01 · What it is

A device that reads
your mind.

NeuroLens is a light pair of smart glasses with a brain sensor built into the sides. It has 64 small sensors that read signals from your brain. These sensors detect what you want to do before your hands move.

A special chip inside the glasses turns those brain signals into actions very fast. The glasses respond to your thoughts.

  • Design

    Made of titanium, only 38 g. It looks like normal glasses.

  • Input

    64 brain sensors + motion sensor + eye tracking at 480 Hz.

  • Output

    Two 4K screens, one for each eye. 120° view. Almost invisible.

  • AI

    An AI model runs inside the glasses. Your data never leaves the device.

02 · Main goal

No more keyboard,
no mouse,
no touchscreen.

Almost all devices need your hands. NeuroLens is different. It reads your brain signals and tracks your eyes. You can use any device — a phone, a computer, or a car — using only your attention.

  • Click, scroll, type, and select using only your thoughts and eyes.
